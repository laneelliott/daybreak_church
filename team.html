LEAD PASTOR

Brit and Stacie Windel

Brit Windel is the founding pastor of DayBreak Church. He met Stacie back in 2009 while she was working for Group Publishing on the Group Mission Trip Team – hiring and training 100+ college students to travel the US and lead home repair and community service projects for 23,000+ participants during the summer.

Brit has a deep desire to help people connect to the life changing story that God is for them and longs for them to have a full-abundant life found only through Jesus Christ. His pastoral journey has taken him from Oklahoma, to Arkansas, to Northern Ireland, to Colorado, and now to Wisconsin.

The Windel’s are excited to be in the community that Stacie grew up in and that her family calls home.

OVERSEERS

Part of DayBreak’s governance is placed not only in elders, but also in pastors outside of this church we call Overseers. Pastor Brit submits himself to these men in all matters for the betterment of himself, his family and this church.

Kevin Pruitt // Kevin’s a Teaching Pastor and Counselor in Aurora, Illinois. Kevin has been Brit’s long time mentor. He was his youth pastor growing up and was instrumental into Brit going into ministry. God reconnected them back in 2012. He knows Brit’s heart and desire to see people come to know Jesus.

Aaron Stern // Aaron’s a Lead Pastor and Church Planter in Fort Collins, Colorado. Aaron and Brit met in spring of 2012. Aaron has been a huge encourager for the Windel’s, DayBreak Church and was one of the men who helped direct us to the Association of Related Church (ARC). Aaron and Mill City Church are incredible supporters of our church!

Matthew Blackford // Matthew’s a Pastor of Respond Ministries in Fort Collins, Colorado. Matthew and Brit 1st met at a pastors gathering for missional minded, sacramental churches. It’s been an incredible blessing to get to know more of Matthew’s heart for God and reaching cities for Jesus. We’re thrilled that Matthew is joining our team of overseers to encourage and keep us focused on the main things!

The main function and role these pastors fill is one of encouragement and accountability to the Pastor and ministry of DayBreak Church.


RISETEAM

As a new church we’re excited that God is sending and stirring you and people like you to join us in this venture to be a life giving church. The RISEteam consists of men and women of all ages and backgrounds who come together to serve this church and this city.
